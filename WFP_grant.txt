With the supplies and equipment that LVFPD requested they will be used for fuel reduction and transportation of tools and heavy equipment. We will use the covered trailer to transport supplies and PPE. We will use the uncovered trailer to transport the green climber to different locations. The supplies include chainsaws for cutting large brush and trees. the weed whackers will be used to clear tall grass and small brush.  The hand tools will be used to manage downed and cut vegetation.  The PPE (personal protective equipment) is specially designed to withstand contact with a chainsaw blade and is also high visibility improving safety for our firefighters we have also requested hard hats.
